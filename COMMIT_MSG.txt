feat: ${stepId} and ${stepName} variables for the current step

The engine now seeds ${stepId} (the current step's id) and ${stepName}
(its name) just before each step runs, next to ${stepDir}. They are
overwritten per step, like stepDir, so inside a step they always refer
to that step. Handy for building values or paths that reference the
current step generically.

Added to the designer cheat sheet and path suggestions, and documented
in USAGE.md / README.

76 classes compile; designer JS valid.
