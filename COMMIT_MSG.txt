ui(designer): clearer collapse (+/-) vs move (arrows) icons

The collapse/expand control used a small ▾ that looked identical to the
step move buttons (▲ / ▼), which was confusing. (The mask step collapse
itself has no bug - the div balance checks out; the caret was just hard
to spot.)

Changes:
- Step-card collapse toggle: bold +/- glyph (- when open, + when
  collapsed), larger and accent-colored.
- Subsection carets (mask and anonymize steps): boxed +/- instead of the
  tiny ▾.
- Move buttons: ▲/▼ replaced with ↑/↓ so "move" is unmistakable and
  distinct from the +/- collapse controls.

The "Workflow files" section header keeps its own caret (separate
control). designer JS valid; no \n/\r; Thymeleaf-safe; no Java change.
